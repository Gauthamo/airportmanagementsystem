AIRPORTS AUTHORITY OF INDIA

The Airports Authority of India or AAI is a Miniratna-1 category Public Sector Enterprise, under the ownership of Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Ministry of Civil Aviation, Government of India. It is responsible for creating, upgrading, maintaining and managing civil aviation infrastructure in India.

It provides Communication Navigation Surveillance / Air Traffic Management (CNS/ATM) services over Indian airspace and adjoining oceanic areas.
